About Me

I first learned to code in my first year of university and over time, have realized just how vast the coding landscape is, which makes me extremely grateful to be studying computer engineering because it has given me the tools and knowledge to create things from scratch. I have most enjoyed learning about computer hardware and machine learning /artificial intelligence.

Some of my favourite things are nature and (almost) all activities and elements associated with the great outdoors like bodies of water, hiking, trails, farms, grass, and animals.

Previously, I was very fortunate to participate in some artistic hobbies, such as playing the flute, and competing in synchronized swimming. I really like to help teach other people and did so through tutoring in music and science subjects at my highschool as well as volunteering in coaching introductory synchronized swimming programs for younger children.
